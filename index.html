Adventure is Out There

Experience our fantastical theme parks for a world of fun.

Disneyland Park

Journey back in time to where it all began. Since opening its gates in 1955, Disneyland Park has invited dreamers to let their imaginations soar. From the iconic It’s a Small World to the spectacular Space Mountain, there’s surely an adventure for all to enjoy.

Disney California Adventure Park

Break out of buildings, corral critters and speed through the skies, all at Disney California Adventure Park. With Radiator Springs Racers, Jumpin’ Jellyfish and the Incredicoaster, endless possibilities are just waiting to be explored.

Find Your Happy Place

Consider us your home away from home.

Grand Californian Hotel and Spa

Live life luxuriously at Disney’s Grand Californian Hotel and Spa. Treat yourself to elegant accommodations, exclusive amenities and fine dining. After a full day of thrills, there's no better way to rest, relax and unwind.

Pixar Place Hotel

Watch your imagination reach new heights with the wonders of Pixar Place Hotel. Finally feel what it’s like to live in the world of your favorite Pixar movies. Get to know the characters from the silver screen like you’ve never seen before.

To Infinity and Beyond

Awe-inspiring thrills and treats await.

Activities

The adventures don’t stop at the attractions! Discover every last drop of magic with character experiences, live entertainment and stage shows. Then, wrap up the festivities with a fireworks spectacle sure to delight.

Dining

Dig in to delicious delicacies for every appetite. Taste all there is to eat: Flo’s Famous Fried Chicken, Bao Buns and much more. With so many treats to try, it’s no surprise if you find yourself hungry for another bite.
